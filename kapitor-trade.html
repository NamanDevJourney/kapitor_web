Module 05 · Risk Engine

Core Governance
Layer

The Risk Engine is the backbone of Kapitor Trade. It governs all trading activity — monitoring seven risk dimensions simultaneously, enforcing controls automatically, and requiring every trade to pass a full risk validation before execution.

⊘

Exposure Concentration Monitoring

Detects over-concentration in single assets, correlated positions, or counterparty exposure clusters.

⊘

Dynamic Margin Adjustment

Margin requirements adjust automatically based on volatility regime, position size, and market conditions.

⊘

Automated Liquidation Logic

Positions approaching threshold trigger automated partial or full liquidation without manual intervention.

⊘

Abnormal Trading Pattern Detection

ML-assisted pattern recognition flags unusual order flows, wash trading signals, and manipulation attempts.

Risk is not a disclaimer — it is enforced by architecture.
