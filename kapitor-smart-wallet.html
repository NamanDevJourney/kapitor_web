Architecture Principles
01

No Off-Ledger Balances

Every asset position — regardless of class — is recorded against the single Unified Ledger. No external reconciliation required.

02

No Internal Shadow Accounting

There are no internal sub-accounts or proxy balances. The ledger is the source. What you see is exactly what is recorded.

03

Real-Time Reconciliation

Every transaction event triggers immediate ledger reconciliation. Balance updates are not batched — they are continuous.

04

Audit-Ready Transaction Mapping

Every transaction generates a Ledger Entry, Counterparty Record, Settlement Confirmation, and Compliance Log automatically.

05

Blockchain-Backed Verification

Where applicable, transaction records are anchored on-chain for immutable verification and proof of settlement.

Transparency is structural, not optional.
