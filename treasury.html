Allocation Engine

Intelligent Deployment.
Measured Exposure.

Treasury capital is routed into Earn strategies, vault structures, trade finance positions, FX hedging, and staking infrastructure — all within defined exposure ceilings, duration limits, and risk thresholds. Every allocation remains fully traceable through the Unified Ledger.

Yield-to-Risk Optimization

Every allocation is evaluated against a yield-to-risk ratio. The engine surfaces optimal deployment paths based on current market conditions, existing exposure, and treasury policy constraints.

Automated Risk Triggers

When exposure thresholds are breached, the Risk Engine can automatically block excessive allocation, require secondary approval, or restrict high-risk positions before execution occurs.

Reserve & Adequacy Monitoring

Capital buffer thresholds, reserve mapping, and liability reconciliation are enforced continuously. Minimum liquidity floors cannot be bypassed by any allocation instruction.

Full Strategy Performance Tracking

Every deployed position reports back yield, maturity, and risk score in real time. Treasury officers see performance breakdown by strategy, asset class, and entity from one interface.
