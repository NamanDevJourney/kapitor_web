Valuation Mechanism

Structured Enterprise
Valuation Mapping

EQOUIN reflects the valuation of Kapitor's underlying enterprise operations across trading infrastructure, yield systems, commodity engine, Kaloft pools, institutional treasury, and payment infrastructure. Valuation is documented — not speculative.

Updates are governed by financial reporting cycles, independent audit review, governance board approval, and ledger-reconciled capital metrics. No arbitrary adjustments.

Ledger-Reconciled Valuation

Every valuation update is reconciled against Unified Ledger capital metrics before it is reflected in the unit price.

Independent Audit Verification

Valuation inputs are independently audited on a quarterly basis. Audit reports are accessible to EQOUIN holders.

Governance Board Approval

No valuation update can be applied without governance board approval. Dilution events and capital structure changes are documented in advance.

No Artificial Inflation

Unit price reflects real enterprise metrics only. There is no market-making, price smoothing, or discretionary adjustment of the EQOUIN unit value.

Valuation is documented — not speculative. NAV movement is tied to real enterprise performance.
Dividend Distribution

Profit-Based Allocation.
Event-Governed.

EQOUIN holders may receive dividend distributions based on net distributable profits. Dividend eligibility depends on profit realization, capital reserves, regulatory constraints, and governance approval — not on a fixed schedule.

Profit-Realization Gating

No dividend is paid unless net distributable profit has been realized, capital reserves are maintained, and governance has approved the distribution.

Multi-Currency Distribution

Dividends may be paid in fiat, stablecoins, or retained as structured earnings depending on governance decision and holder preference.

Profit Source Attribution

Each dividend is attributed to its source — trading infrastructure, yield systems, commodity engine, vault performance, or institutional flows. Full transparency per distribution event.

Per-Unit Transparency

Dividend per unit, total distributed, distribution date, and profit source attribution are all disclosed and ledger-recorded for every distribution event.

Dividends are event-based and governed. Not scheduled. Not guaranteed. Tied to enterprise profit realization.

Internal Trading Market

Controlled Liquidity.
No External Listing.

EQOUIN trades exclusively within the Kapitor internal marketplace — accessible only through compliance-tier-approved accounts. Liquidity is structured and governed. There is no external listing dependency, no external exchange volatility.

◈

Compliance Tier Access

EQOUIN trading is gated by compliance tier. Eligibility is verified at account level before market access is granted.

◈

Limit & Market Orders

Full order book trading with limit orders, market orders, bid-ask visibility, and transaction history — within the internal marketplace.

◈

Settlement via Unified Ledger

All trades settle through the Unified Ledger. Each trade generates an ownership transfer record, ledger entry, compliance log, and updated circulating supply figure.

◈

Liquidity Governance

Market liquidity is actively managed. There are no uncontrolled flash prices — the Liquidity Governance system enforces order size limits and spread controls.

No external listing dependency. Liquidity is structured and governed. All trades settle via Unified Ledger with full compliance logging.
