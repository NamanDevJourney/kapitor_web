Proof of Reserves

User Balances Are Not
Abstract Entries.

Every balance held inside Kapitor is mapped to a corresponding reserve — fiat balances, digital assets, Kaloft pool capital, RWA custody allocations, and institutional instrument collateral. Reserve verification is on-ledger, continuous, and independently confirmed.

On-Ledger Asset Verification

Every customer asset position is verified against the Unified Ledger in real time. No balance exists without a corresponding reserve entry.

Liability Reconciliation

Total customer liabilities are reconciled against reserve holdings continuously. Discrepancies trigger automatic review — not periodic audits.

Institutional Instrument Collateral

KFI instruments, lending facilities, and Prime institutional positions maintain collateral ratios above minimum thresholds. Current blended ratio: 128%.

Audit Reference Reporting

Proof of Reserves audit reports are published quarterly and available on-demand to institutional clients through Kapitor Prime transparency tools.

User balances are mapped to reserves. No shadow accounting. No unmapped balances. Full traceability.
