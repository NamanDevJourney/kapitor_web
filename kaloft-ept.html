Economic Participation Model

Structured Capital
Participation

Kaloft allows users to allocate capital into predefined participation pools that fund Kapitor's core infrastructure — trading, commodities, yield deployment, treasury, and institutional settlement. Capital is locked for a defined duration. NAV grows based on pool performance. All capital flows are reconciled with the Unified Ledger.

◈

Pool ID & Entry NAV

Each participant receives a unique Pool ID with entry NAV locked at time of allocation.

◈

Lock Duration Selected at Entry

1Y, 3Y, or 5Y. Duration is fixed at participation. Cannot be changed after capital is committed.

◈

Redemption NAV Entitlement

Maturity value is calculated from entry NAV × participation multiplier, subject to pool performance and compliance conditions.

◈

No Variable Promises

Returns are structured, not promised. All participation terms are documented prior to entry and cannot be modified.

◈

Position Visible in Smart Wallet

NAV movement is tied exclusively to real infrastructure activity. No synthetic adjustments, no hidden smoothing.

Redemption Framework

Structured Maturity
Settlement

At maturity, the lock period ends, final NAV is calculated, the participation multiplier is applied, and funds are released to the Smart Wallet. Participants may redeem in fiat, stablecoins, or approved crypto assets.

Fiat Redemption

Receive maturity value in USD, EUR, GBP, or other supported fiat currencies via GIPS payment rails.

Stablecoin Redemption

Receive in USDT, USDC, or other approved stablecoins directly to Smart Wallet balance.

Crypto Asset Redemption

Receive in BTC, ETH, or other approved digital assets at prevailing market rate at maturity.

Ledger Settlement Records

Every redemption generates: ledger settlement record, reserve mapping update, compliance log, and tax reporting record where applicable.
