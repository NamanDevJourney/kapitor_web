Live Infrastructure

Every Payment. Routed. Recorded.
Reconciled.

GIPS processes payments across seven distinct rail types — from sub-2-second UPI transfers to multi-day SWIFT wires — under a single governance layer. Every transaction follows the same 7-step sequence regardless of rail, corridor, or currency.

Compliance-First Architecture

Compliance screening runs before any payment is routed. High-risk transactions trigger review workflows — no payment bypasses governance regardless of rail or currency.

FX Transparency

Applied exchange rate, spread, fee breakdown, and settlement time are visible to every user on every cross-currency transaction. No opaque conversion markup.

Unified Ledger Reconciliation

Every GIPS payment creates a ledger entry at the moment of execution — not at settlement. Reserve reconciliation is immediate and continuous.

No Off-Ledger Movement

No GIPS payment moves without a corresponding ledger entry and reserve mapping. There are no undocumented flows, no shadow balances, and no rail that operates outside governance.

Governance & Risk

No Rail Bypasses Governance.
Every Transaction Screened.

⊘
AML Monitoring

Every payment is screened against AML rules before routing. High-risk patterns trigger review workflows — flow is paused, not failed.

§
Jurisdiction-Based Restrictions

Each rail operates under jurisdiction-specific rules. Access is automatically gated by the sender's compliance tier and destination jurisdiction.

◈
Transaction Limit Enforcement

Per-rail, per-corridor, and per-entity transaction limits are enforced at routing — not flagged post-execution. Limits cannot be bypassed.

▣
Sanctions Screening

All counterparties are screened against active sanctions lists before each payment. Sanctions matches block routing automatically.

⬡
Risk Scoring

Transaction risk scores are calculated at entry. Risk score updates feed the Risk Engine continuously — no static scoring between transactions.

⇄
Reserve Reconciliation

Reserve mapping is the final step of every payment. No GIPS payment is complete without confirmed reserve reconciliation in the Unified Ledger.
