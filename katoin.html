Commodity Tokenization

Real Quantity.
Real Price. Ledger Verified.

Katoin tokens represent verified commodity quantity, entry price, storage or transit status, and trade lifecycle stage. Tokenization occurs only after five sequential verification steps — no unbacked issuance is possible.

Each token carries complete metadata: commodity type, lot size, purchase value, trade route, expected margin range, and settlement window. All data is recorded inside the Unified Ledger.

No Unbacked Issuance

Every Katoin token is backed by a verified, physically confirmed commodity lot. Token supply cannot exceed verified physical inventory.

Trade Lifecycle Tracking

Each token tracks the full lifecycle from sourcing to final sale — storage status, transit stage, trade level, and distribution readiness.

Counterparty-Validated

Every trade involves counterparty validation through KYB/KYC screening before the lot is accepted into the Katoin framework.

No unbacked issuance. All data is recorded inside the Unified Ledger.

Profit Distribution

Automated Upon
Commercial Sale

Profit is distributed only when commodity is sold, payment is confirmed, settlement is cleared, and margin is verified. No pre-promised yield. Profit is event-based — triggered by real commercial execution, not by time or schedule.

Event-Based Profit

No yield schedule. No estimated returns. Profit flows when and only when a commercial sale is executed and payment is cleared.

Transparent Cost Basis

Entry price, sale price, gross margin, and net profit are all visible to Katoin holders at the lot level — no opacity in the margin calculation.

Ledger-Recorded Distributions

Every distribution is ledger-recorded, reserve-mapped, and audit-tracked. The timestamp, amount, and lot reference are permanently stored.

Multi-Currency Settlement

Distributions may settle in fiat, stablecoins, or approved crypto assets via GIPS payment rails and Kapitor Smart Wallet.
