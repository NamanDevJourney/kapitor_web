Compliance & Governance

Regulated Access.
Controlled Risk.

Every user operates within structured regulatory boundaries. Product access is determined by compliance tier — automatically enforced at the infrastructure level.

01 / KYC

Know Your Customer

Automated identity verification with biometric, document, and sanctions screening. Completed in under 60 seconds with real-time status updates.

02 / KYB

Know Your Business

Entity verification for corporate accounts. UBO mapping, corporate structure analysis, and regulatory status confirmation across jurisdictions.

03 / AML

AML Monitoring

Real-time transaction monitoring with ML-powered risk scoring. FATF Travel Rule compliant. Continuous screening against global watchlists.

04 / RISK

Risk Profiling

Dynamic user and transaction risk profiles updated continuously. Scores govern investment limits and product access in real time.

05 / GEO

Jurisdiction Controls

Granular country-level access controls. Regulatory-driven restrictions enforced at the infrastructure layer — not at the UI level.

06 / LIMITS

Investment Limits

Compliance-tier-based investment thresholds enforced automatically. Limits update as user verification level progresses.

Product access is determined by compliance tier. Every user operates within structured regulatory boundaries.
