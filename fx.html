FX Architecture

Integrated Liquidity
& Settlement Engine.

Kapitor FX connects regional banking rails, international settlement systems, institutional liquidity pools, and the treasury routing engine. Every conversion passes through six governed steps. No untracked conversions. No hidden markup.

Corporate FX Routing

Treasury officers can pre-approve currency corridors, set maximum spread tolerance, enable FX hedging, and lock conversion policies. All treasury conversions update capital adequacy ratios in real time.

Liquidity Management

Corridor liquidity thresholds, per-currency exposure caps, and real-time volatility monitoring govern all flows. High-value conversions may trigger secondary authorization or liquidity verification delay.

Multi-Currency Conversion Support

Fiat-to-fiat, fiat-to-stablecoin, stablecoin-to-fiat, cross-currency treasury routing, and multi-entity capital rebalancing. Currency pair availability governed by jurisdiction, compliance tier, and liquidity.

GIPS Settlement Integration

Every FX conversion routes directly into GIPS rails — domestic payments, international wires, card networks, trade finance instruments — with a single ledger entry covering debit, credit, spread, and compliance log.
