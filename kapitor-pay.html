Module 03 · Cards & Virtual Cards

Controlled Spending
Infrastructure

Kapitor issues four card types — Physical Debit, Virtual, Multi-Currency, and Corporate Treasury — each with granular spend controls and ledger-level settlement.

Real-Time Freeze / Unfreeze

Suspend or activate any card instantly via the Smart Wallet dashboard or API.

Merchant Category Restrictions

Restrict spend by MCC code — essential for corporate treasury and employee card programmes.

Geographic Restrictions

Enable or restrict card use by country, region, or IP zone in real time.

Virtual Card Generation

Instantly generate single-use or recurring virtual cards for online spend with custom limits.

Settlement Logic — Cards settle against
Fiat balance (direct deduction)
Converted crypto (real-time FX conversion)
Yield-released funds (from eligible positions)
